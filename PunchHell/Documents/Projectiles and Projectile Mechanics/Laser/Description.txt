-ChargeTime:
3 seconds. During this time, show the player a projection of where the laser is. This projection CAN move towards the player, however once firing, the laser CAN NO LONGER MOVE.

-Duration:
2 seconds.

-Speed:
12 frames. Should fill up the screen in 100 or less MS.

-Targeting Speed:
Slow. The player should be able to easily straif away from the laser. 1/2 player movement speed for adjusting the positioning.

Damage: Depends on difficulty.
	Easy: 1/4 heart
	Medium: 1/2 heart
	Hard: 1 heart
	Impossible: DEATH
